Features

The most powerful tools for on-chain development and data science.

Unified Multi-Chain Development

Build, test, and deploy across local forks, testnets, and live networks—simultaneously. Ape’s unified command-line workflow removes context switching so you can script, debug, and ship to any chain without reconfiguring your environment.

Modular Plugin Ecosystem

Extend Ape into the exact toolkit your project needs. With first-party and community plugins—from Solidity, Vyper, Starknet, and ENS, to QuickNode, Alchemy, Hardhat, Foundry, and Ledger—Ape turns into a customizable Web3 workstation.

End-to-End Contract Lifecycle Automation

Compile, test, deploy, analyze, and verify your smart contracts with a single framework. Ape provides package manifest generation, ABI tools, structured scripting, and a rich interactive console for full lifecycle management.

Production-Grade Debugging & Tracing

See exactly what your contracts are doing. Ape includes transaction tracing, gas profiling, revert detection, and decoded calldata output—giving developers full visibility during both development and live-network debugging.

Secure Account Management

Protect your on-chain operations with encrypted keyfiles, hardware-wallet support, and flexible signing flows. ApeSigner and AccountAPI give you enterprise-grade account security inside your Python development stack.

Powered by Python’s Scientific Ecosystem

Ape integrates directly with Jupyter Notebooks, Pandas, NumPy, and the entire Python data science ecosystem. Analyze on-chain data, model transactions, or prototype trading algorithms in the same environment where you deploy.
